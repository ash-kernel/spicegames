Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Our Commitment

SpiceDeck is built on a simple principle: your privacy is non-negotiable. We believe your gaming library and usage patterns are your personal information, and they should stay that way.

What We Don't Collect

SpiceDeck does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or authentication systems
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No telemetry or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party trackers
  • No cloud storage or data synchronization

How Your Data is Stored

All application data is stored locally on your device:

  • Game library data is stored in local database files on your computer
  • User preferences and settings are saved in local configuration files
  • Game metadata (covers, ratings) is cached locally after being fetched from public APIs
  • Playtime statistics are tracked and stored entirely on your device

Third-Party Services

SpiceDeck may fetch data from third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Game metadata APIs to retrieve covers, ratings, and completion times
  • Gaming news sources (PC Gamer, IGN) for news feeds
  • Store APIs (Steam, Epic, GOG, Humble) for deal information

These requests are made directly from your device to the respective services. We do not proxy or monitor these requests. Each service has its own privacy policy and terms of service.

Internet Connectivity

SpiceDeck can function completely offline. Internet connectivity is only required for:

  • Fetching game covers and metadata for newly added games
  • Viewing current game deals from stores
  • Reading gaming news feeds

All core functionality (launching games, viewing library, tracking playtime) works without an internet connection.

Open Source

SpiceDeck is open source software. You can review the entire codebase on GitHub to verify that we do not collect or transmit personal data. The source code is available at github.com/ash-kernel/spicedeck.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of SpiceDeck after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the developer at github.com/ash-kernel.
